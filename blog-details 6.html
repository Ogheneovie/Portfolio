The Role of SEO in Web Development: Building Websites That Rank

  • news
  • Dec 12th, 2023
  • admin

"Imagine you have a lemonade stand.
You want everyone to know about your yummy lemonade, right? A website is like a big sign telling people about your stand.

It shows what flavors of lemonade you have, where you're located, and when you're open. Now, imagine your lemonade stand is open all the time and people from everywhere can visit, even when you're sleeping! That's what a website does for a business. It stays open 24/7 and tells people all the cool things about your business, like what you sell and how they can reach you.

“The power of the Web is in its universality.
Access by everyone regardless of disability
is an essential aspect.”

Tim Berners-Lee (The Inventor of the World Wide Web)

"Just like your lemonade stand needs a good sign to bring in customers, a business needs a website to let people know they exist and what they offer. It helps lots of people find them and become happy customers!"

Remember, a website is like a big sign that never sleeps, telling everyone about your business all the time!.
